Community Offerings

Jefferson County Offerings for Families with Little Ones:

​

Scribbles

This class is designed for children 1 year and older with caregiver participation. We play in water, explore taste-safe art and sensory materials, make take-home projects, strengthen fine and gross motor skills, and socializing in a beautiful, light-filled space. 

- Monday's 10:00AM-11:00AM

-$25 drop-in or subscribe and save 20%

​

Tumble & Talk

- Thursdays 10:00AM - 11:30AM

- Play, stretch and roll time for parents and littles. For ages 2 and under or children up to 3 who are gentle with younger children.

- $5 drop in or $15-$20 sliding scale for the month. Cash or Venmo "Jessica-Tartaro".

- Because this is a parent-run group, sometimes we start a little late. Please wait, we always get there eventually!

- For questions, contact Jessica at 480-993-5562

 

Story Time at the Port Townsend Library:

-Baby Story Time: This 20-minute lapsit program for babies ages 0-18 months features short stories, action rhymes, lap bounces, and songs to promote language learning and early literacy skills. Parents and caregivers will find a supportive community and opportunities for engagement and connection. Playtime to follow.

-Mondays at 10:30

-Toddler Story Time: For children 18 months to 3 years. Tuesdays at 10:30

 

Story Time at the Jefferson County Library in Port Hadlock:

For infants and early toddlers. Join us for songs, rhymes, simple stories, and playful movements designed to stimulate brain development and joyful learning. An optional open playtime after the program is a great opportunity for your little ones to interact and socialize. 

-Wednesdays at 10:30

 

Kaleidoscope Play and Learn:

Playgroup for parents, caregivers and kids ages zero to five years old. Stories, crafts, circle time, and supportive discussions. Play groups are free and open to everyone! Fridays at 10 am

For more information call the First Step Family Support Center (360) 457-8355

 

PT Playschool Co-op:

A cooperative facilitated playgroup located at the Port Townsend Recreation Center with activities, snacks, and parent education. Must pre-register for each quarter.

Fridays at 9:30 am for 9 months to 4 years

https://ptplayschool.org/

 

YMCA of Jefferson County Family Navigation Program:

Family Resource Navigators, Tanya Barnett, Rowen DeLuna, and Molly French work to help families connect with the resources they need. 

Phone: 360-565-6025

Email: tanya@olympicpeninsulaymca.org,  frnconnect@olympicpeninsulymca.org
